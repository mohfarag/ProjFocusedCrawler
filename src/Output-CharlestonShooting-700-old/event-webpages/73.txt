				BREAKING REPORTS: Mass Shooting In N. Charleston South Carolina…			

				06/17/2015			


fuzzysdad01 

Uncategorized 

Leave a comment 


 Originally posted on The Last Refuge:
Sketchy details, but possibly 8 people deceased.  In Charleston SC there is a MASSIVE response, including confirmation of FBI, to a mass casualty shooting event being described as taking place in a church.    Initial ground reports are the LEO response units are severely distressed at what has been found so far.
The shooting took place at 9:00pm.  The Church as been identified at 110 Calhoun Street, it is the AME church of Charleston. (website here) Shooter still on the loose.
