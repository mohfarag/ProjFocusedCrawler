Police: Officer Shot Suspect Armed with Knife Near Cesar Chavez Park

Posted 7:35 PM, May 21, 2015, by Genny McLaren 

FacebookTwitterRedditGooglePocketTumblrEmail 


SACRAMENTO-
A man allegedly armed with a knife was shot by Sacramento police near Cesar Chavez Park in Sacramento on Thursday night, according to investigators.
The shooting took place on the 900 block of I Street, near Cesar Chavez Park.
The man was transported to an area hospital, according to police. His condition is not known at this time.
No officers were injured in the incident.
I Street is closed to through traffic between 9th and 10th streets. Drivers are advised to use caution in the area as emergency crews are present.
No further details were released.
Stay with FOX40 and FOX40.com for updates on this developing story.
