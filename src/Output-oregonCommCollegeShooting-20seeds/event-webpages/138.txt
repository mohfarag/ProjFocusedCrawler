Related Coverage


















































ROSEBURG, Ore. (MEDIA GENERAL) -– From presidential candidates to music and television stars, the mass shooting in Oregon has people reacting.


A gunman opened fire at an Oregon community college Thursday, 
, authorities said.


The shooting happened at Umpqua Community College in Roseburg, about 180 miles south of Portland.


Many prominent names took to Twitter to express condolences, thoughts and prayers. Others used the moment to discuss gun control. Many Twitter users, both famous and not, are using the hashtag #StandwithUCC.
