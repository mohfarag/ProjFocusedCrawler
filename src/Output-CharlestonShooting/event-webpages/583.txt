Modesto Police Name Suspect After Man’s Body Found in Garage

Posted 7:15 PM, June 18, 2015, by Genny McLaren 

FacebookTwitterRedditGooglePocketTumblrEmail 


MODESTO —
The Modesto Police Department have identified a suspect who is believed to have been involved in the death of a man found in a garage.
William Smith (Courtesy: Modesto Police Dept.)
Detectives are looking for 44-year-old William Smith. He is suspected in the murder of Kenneth Sedra Jr., whose body was found in a triplex garage this week.
Smith is considered armed and dangerous.
He is described as white, 6-feet-1-inch tall, weighing about 210 pounds. He has brown hair, that is possibly shaved, blue eyes and multiple tattoos on his arms and chest.
If you know his whereabouts, contact Crime Stoppers at (209) 521-4636. Callers can remain anonymous and may be eligible for a cash reward.
