                Results of the Uttar Pradesh Class 10 examination will be declared by the UP Board  today on May 17th in a few hours time. 
               




 


 
 
 
Results of the Uttar Pradesh Class 10 examination will be declared by the UP Board  today on May 17th in a few hours time. 
The result would be available on the board’s official website  upmsp.nic.in and upresults.nic.in Candidates need to log on to any one of the website for results. This year the exam was completed on March 11th. Finally the long wait of examinees will end with the publication of results. The candidates can download the pdf of their result and keep a printout for future use.
About the board: 
The Board was set up in the year 1921 at Allahabad by an act of United Provinces Legislative Council. It conducted its first examination in 1923. According the board's website, at present the Board is the biggest examining body in the World.
