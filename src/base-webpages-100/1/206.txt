Boko Haram kidnaps 80 people in Cameroon
At least 80 people were kidnapped by militant group Boko Haram in northern Cameroon, as per media reports. The kidnappings occurred in the villages of Mabass and Makxy in the Mayo-Tsanaga Division of the Far North Region of Cameroon. Three of the kidnapped people were found murdered. Meanwhile Chad has sent thousands of troops to Cameroon to help the country in its fight against Boko Haram. WBPB1490115
