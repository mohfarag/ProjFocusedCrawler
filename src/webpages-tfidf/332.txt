Parents of missing University of Virginia student Hannah Graham release statement

The search for missing University of Virginia student Hannah Graham has been extended into northern Nelson County based on a new lead.













UPDATE: Jesse Matthew's reckless driving hearing continued to Dec. 1

Matthew's attorney, Jim Camblos, confirmed Wednesday that he will not ask for Matthew to be released on bond.













Sexual assault investigated while Matthew was student at Christopher Newport University

Christopher Newport University officials confirmed today that Jesse Matthew, the man accused of abducting UVA student Hannah Graham, was investigated for sexual assault on the campus in 2003.













UVA student starting program that would walk students around grounds

Jack Capra is from Vienna, Virginia, and says his friends and he were talking about campus safety after Hannah Graham first was reported missing almost three weeks ago.













UVA student speaks out about Jesse Matthew 

One student at UVA, who also worked at the UVa. medical center where Jesse Matthew worked, says she has been on guard for five years, ever since the Morgan Harrington murder.
