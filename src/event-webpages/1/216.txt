Views:








Your college football playoff selections are here, and Alabama, Oregon, Florida State, and Ohio State will face off to decide this season's national champion.
The College Football Playoff, or some other postseason game, is probably the only you'll see these teams on the field together.
The College Football Playoff selection committee revealed its first four-team bracket on Sunday, seeding Alabama No. 1, Oregon No. 2, Florida State No. 3 and Ohio State No. 4.   A 12-1 record, a Big Ten title and 59 points Saturday night was enough to make Ohio State one of the four.
Ohio State's convincing 59-0 win over Wisconsin in the Big Ten title game last night served as an impressive closing salvo in its argument to be included in the College Football Playoff.
